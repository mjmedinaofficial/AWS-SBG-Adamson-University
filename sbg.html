Student Builder Groups

Student Builder Groups (SBGs) are student-led communities, supported by AWS, where learners come together to grow their cloud skills, build real projects, and connect with a global network of builders.

Student-Led AWS-Supported Hands-On Learning Global Network

A Student Builder Group is a community of students who share a passion for cloud computing — organized by students, for students, with support from AWS and a worldwide network of mentors.

What It Is

A welcoming space to learn AWS hands-on, exchange ideas, and turn curiosity into in-demand cloud skills.

What We Run

Workshops, study sessions, and build challenges — each group sets its own pace, guided by AWS resources and community mentors.

Who It's For

Anyone starting their cloud journey at Adamson — no matter your course, year level, or prior experience with AWS.

"It's a place to start your cloud journey, no matter your background or year level — built by students who believe cloud skills should be open to everyone."

Why Join a Builder Group?

Learn

Get hands-on with AWS services through guided labs, study groups, and certification prep — at a pace that works for students.

Build

Apply what you learn by building real projects on the cloud, from your first web app to serverless and AI/ML experiments.

Connect

Meet like-minded peers, mentors, and AWS community builders. Grow your network locally and across the global community.

Grow

Develop leadership, collaboration, and technical skills that set you apart — and open doors to internships and careers in tech.
