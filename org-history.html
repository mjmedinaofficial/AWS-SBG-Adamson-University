Student-Led Cloud & AI Community-Driven Builders for All

Formerly known as AWS Cloud Club, we are now the AWS Student Builder Group – Adamson University — a broader vision that extends beyond cloud computing to embrace today's dynamic tech landscape, where students build with cloud and AI alike.

What is AWS SBG?

Our name reflects a vision that reaches beyond cloud alone. Our community, history, service, and commitment to empowering students remain the same — now welcoming all learners from every skill bracket.

Organization History

AWS Cloud Club Adamson University was officially founded on October 2, 2024, by Kate Danielle Callao — driven by a vision to bring AWS and cloud computing education to every Adamson student. Like all founding chapters, the first term was a period of building, learning, and laying groundwork that would carry the org forward. Under Kelvin Ignacio's presidency, that foundation grew into something recognized — rising as a top socio-civic organization and eventually evolving into AWS Student Builder Group, opening its doors to all learners regardless of skill level, with one goal: to transform students into builders.

Vision & Mission

We build a lasting, nationwide community of passionate learners who harness AWS to drive innovation — empowering students through workshops, networking, volunteerism, and bridges to the AWS ecosystem.

Continuing its vision to upskill students with competitive skills in cloud and AI, the organization transitioned into AWS Student Builder Group — transforming learners from every skill bracket into builders.
