Mission

To make AWS and cloud computing accessible to every Adamson student through hands-on workshops, peer-led learning, and a supportive community built on the belief:

"By the students, for the students, and with the students."
Vision

A campus where every Adamsonian can confidently build on the cloud — connected to mentors, industry opportunities, and a global network of AWS Student Builders.

About Our Community

Connect with peers, mentors, and industry guest speakers while building real cloud skills and earning shared success. This is where the AWS Student Builder Group identity comes alive through community, events, and practical learning.

Join us and be part of a growing network of cloud builders at Adamson University.
