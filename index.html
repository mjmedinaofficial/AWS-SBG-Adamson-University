What is AWS Student Builder Group?

#StudentLed #CloudAI #CommunityDriven #BuildersForAll

Formerly known as AWS Cloud Club, we are now known as the AWS Student Builder Group – Adamson University. This name reflects a broader vision that extends beyond cloud computing to embrace the full range of today's dynamic tech landscape — where students build not only with cloud but with AI as well.

Our name might have changed, but our community, history, service to the community, and commitment to empowering students still remain the same.

It's still day one.

Organization History

#Founded2024 #Community

AWS Cloud Club Adamson University was officially founded on October 2, 2024, by Kate Danielle Callao — driven by a vision to bring AWS and cloud computing education to every Adamson student. Like all founding chapters, the first term was a period of building, learning, and laying groundwork that would carry the org forward.

Under Kelvin Ignacio's presidency, that foundation grew into something recognized — rising as a top socio-civic organization and eventually evolving into AWS Student Builder Group, opening its doors to all learners regardless of skill level, with one goal: to transform students into builders.

Vision & Mission

#Vision #Mission #Innovation

Vision

To build a lasting, nationwide community of passionate learners who embrace innovation and harness the power of AWS to drive technological advancement.

Mission

We empower students to grow into their chosen fields through inclusive education, hands-on workshops, and meaningful networking — equipping them with the skills to thrive in the digital economy.

We champion volunteerism and community service, creating real opportunities for students to give back. And we serve as the bridge between students and the AWS ecosystem, preparing them to help transform industries through cloud technology.
